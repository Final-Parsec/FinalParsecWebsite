About

Final Parsec is an up and coming indie game development studio based in North Texas.

Our team was originally brought together by the legendary TJ Brosnan to develop Space Snakes, a 2 dimensional action space shooter arena. Our game development team consists of Matt Bauer, Baer Bradford, and TJ Brosnan.

At Final Parsec, we look to pioneer the future of game development and publishing.

We have worked with graphic designers Stephen Wilson and Mi Sa Ho for a portion of our artwork. In addition, we have incorporated assets from open source projects and those generous enough to provide their work to the world. We make an effort to give back to the community which has helped us so much. So if you're a fellow developer or interested in the process, we encourage you to take a look at our tutorials.

We released our first game, Nauticus, in August 2014. It is a three game mini-series about piracy, love, and zombies.
(source | tutorials)

Our second release, Aurora Tower Defense, is our first video game available as an Android app via the Google Play Store. Aurora TD is a maze based tower defense game that depicts the Inuit people's battle against the demons of the North to save the Northern Lights.
